Site Purpose

The Hayden Chamber of Commerce is a voice for local business owners and leaders in the community. Our mission is to create an atmosphere of economic growth by providing business-to-business marketing opportunities as well as developing future business leaders. We provide essential support to chamber members that enable them to meet their established goals.
